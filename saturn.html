Saturn: The Ringed Planet

Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System. It is a gas giant with an average radius about nine times that of Earth. Saturn is best known for its prominent ring system, which is composed mostly of ice particles, with a smaller amount of rocky debris and dust.

Did you know?

Saturn has 82 official moons!
