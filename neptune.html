Planet Neptune

The farthest planet from the Sun in our solar system. Neptune, the eighth planet from the Sun and the fourth largest in size and third largest in mass. It is a gaseous giant with a deep blue color due to the presence of methane in its atmosphere. Neptune has a dynamic atmosphere with strong winds and storms, including the Great Dark Spot, which is similar to Jupiter's Great Red Spot. It has 14 known moons, with Triton being the largest and most interesting due to its retrograde orbit and geologic activity. Neptune was discovered in 1846 based on mathematical predictions rather than direct observation, making it a significant milestone in astronomy.
