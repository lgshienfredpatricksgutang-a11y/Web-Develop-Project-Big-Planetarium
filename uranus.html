Planet Uranus

The seventh planet from the Sun and the third-largest in our solar system. Uranus is an ice giant with a blue-green color due to the presence of methane in its atmosphere. It has a unique rotation, as it rotates on its side with an axial tilt of about 98 degrees. This means that its poles experience extreme seasons, with each pole getting around 42 years of continuous sunlight followed by 42 years of darkness.
