Planet Mercury

The smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun. Mercury, the innermost planet of the solar system and the eighth in size and mass. Its closeness to the Sun and its smallness make it the most elusive of the planets visible to the unaided eye. Because its rising or setting is always within about two hours of the Sun's, it is never observable when the sky is fully dark.
