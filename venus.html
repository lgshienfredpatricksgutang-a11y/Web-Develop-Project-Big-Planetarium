Planet Venus

The second planet from the Sun and the hottest planet in our solar system. Venus is often called Earth's "sister planet" because of their similar size and composition. However, Venus has a thick atmosphere composed mainly of carbon dioxide, with clouds of sulfuric acid, making it inhospitable to life as we know it. The surface temperature on Venus can reach up to 900 degrees Fahrenheit (475 degrees Celsius), which is hot enough to melt lead.
